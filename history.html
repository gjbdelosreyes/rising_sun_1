HISTORY

Rising Sun Wood Products Inc. is a manufacturing company based in Tunasan, Muntinlupa and is one of the fastest growing maker and supplier of quality wood products. Throughout the years, the company has been expanding its product lines to cater to the needs of its ever expanding customer base. From starting its door line back in 2009, the company now offers a wide assortment of cost-effective products that include door jambs, flooring, stair components, decorative mouldings, panelling, cabinetry components, and modular systems. In keeping up with a diverse range of customer demand for wood products, the company also offers product customization to meet the very specific needs and requirements of its customers.

As the company is dedicated to fulfil the ever changing needs of its clients, it has embraced innovation by investing heavily in German technology for its manufacturing facilities. Foremost of which is the investment in a fully automated and computerized Kiln Drying facility from Germany that has a monthly capacity of 1,200,000 board feet. The company also continues to stay abreast with new processes and technologies to ensure that existing products are improved with new designs and new products are continuously introduced to the market. With a strong focus on great customer experiences, the company also takes pride in customizing products according to clients’ specifications. The company’s workforce also shares in its vision of providing high quality products and great customer service. Serving customers is simply a shared passion that drives the organization’s day to day operations.

In staying true to its commitment to serve, Rising Sun Wood Products Inc. produces roughly 500,000 board feet of mouldings, 12,000 pieces of wooden doors and 10,000 pieces of door jambs every month. As its market share continues to rise, the company will continue to focus on its efforts in improving its products and services by strategically investing in workforce training, new processes and new technologies.
